Overview

The United States Security & Exchange Commission (SEC) recommends that you always ask for, and carefully read, both parts of Form ADV before you hire someone to be your investment advisor. Form ADV, in two parts, provides essential information about investment advisors' business strategies, regulatory compliance history, service offerings and associated costs of advisory programs.
